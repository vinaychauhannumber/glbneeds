# 🚀 GLB Needs

> An All-in-One Educational Resource Platform for Students

---

## 📌 Overview

**GLB Needs** is a centralized platform designed to help students access all their academic resources in one place.  
From notes and syllabus to previous year papers and AI assistance — everything is just a click away.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📚 **Subject-wise Notes**
- 🎥 **Lecture Videos**
- 📄 **Syllabus Overview**
- 📝 **Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs)**
- ⬇️ **Downloadable Resources**
- 🤖 **AI Chatbot Assistant**
- 🔐 **User Authentication (Supabase)**
- ⚡ **Fast & Responsive UI**

---

## 🧠 Problem It Solves

Students often struggle to find reliable study materials scattered across different platforms.  
GLB Needs solves this by providing:

- A **centralized hub**
- **Organized academic content**
- **Easy accessibility**
- **Time-saving navigation**
